Call for Sponsorship

WebCamp Zagreb invites you to become a sponsor for our annual WebCamp Zagreb 2014 Conference which will be held at the Conference center Hypo Expo XXI on October 4, 2014.

Started in 2012 by local user groups to promote the exchange of knowledge and experience in healthy cooperation between communities, WebCamp managed to attract visitors from neighbouring countries and the wider region, reaching over 600 visitors last year.

While WebCamp Zagreb is focused on the wider region and international audience, local user groups will also organize the Week of Webcamp, a series of events in the days before main event.

Sponsor this event to gain visibility with our audience and enjoy the fact that your sponsorship will help assure the success of WebCamp Zagreb 2014.
