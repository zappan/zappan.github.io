Supporter packs

Continuing with the tradition to provide free tickets and enable everybody to join our ever growing community, this year we are introducing the Supporter packs - the awesome in small bags!

Beyond a cool t-shirt and "larger than life" stickers, other goodies will also be included in this small bag. Although we all love t-shirts and stickers, you'll really be helping us move this conference in a new direction and make it better for everybody.

Also, cool swag is cool ;-)

Of course, a conference ticket is included with the other goodies in the Supporter pack.
