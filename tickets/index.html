WebCamp 2014, Zagreb, October 4th & 5th, 2014

Technology oriented conference for
developers & designers

⋆ October 4th & 5th, 2014 • Hypo Expo XXI • Slavonska ave. 6 • Zagreb ⋆

WebCamp Zagreb is a tech conference built by community for the community. With more than 600 web developers & designers attending last years conference, the concept of sharing experiences and knowledge across different communities in South East Europe becomes even more apparent. This year all talks are in English.

Conference Tickets

  • Free Conference tickets: available on September 11th, at 9:00
  • Supporter Packs: available till September 10th, at 23:59
  • Tickets URL: Entrio.hr

Free tickets will be distributed through Entrio.hr, and are limited to a single ticket per person. Supporter Pack sales ends the evening before, and will not be available for purchase after that time. As promised earlier, the minimum guaranteed number of free tickets getting released is 200. Please, make sure you create your account at Entrio.hr in advance, so that you’re all ready and set when tickets become available.

Workshops

  • Workshop tickets: available on September 11th, at 10:00
  • Tickets URL: Entrio.hr

WebCamp Zagreb offers you six workshops to choose from, details and schedule available at the workshops page.

Week of WebCamp Events

Tickets for Week of WebCamp events on Wednesday and Thursday will be distributed through the involved communities’ channels: JsZgb, PythonHr, Ruby Zg and ZgPHP, respectively.

Week of WebCamp events, including workshops, will be held at HGK (Croatian Chamber of Commerce) building, Nova Cesta 3-7, Zagreb.
